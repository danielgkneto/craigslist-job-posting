The 'Magic!' link populate the table with fake jobs, for testing purposes.

The search funcionality applies to title and author only. Words separated by spaces are all searched for in both title and author (word1 OR word2 OR ...).

Most of the layout is responsive in some extent, including the table.

The 'date posted' field is filled automatically with the current date when adding a new job.

All the columns in the table truncate with ellipsis in the end, after a max width is reached.

There are tooltips over the 'Actions' buttons in the table.
